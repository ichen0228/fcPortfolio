Project #1 Lost in Translation

Description

This is a drawing I made from shape primative with p5.js.

Design Process

I started by drawing with Google Sheets, putting the screenshot of the outcome into illustrator to map up a grid, and then duplicating the forms with code.

Reflections

Comparing to when I was guesstimating all the coordinates in p5, it helped alot to mapping out where the coordinates are with illustrator beforhand, and this additional step made putting geometry on p5 more intuitive.
