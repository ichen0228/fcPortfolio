Project #7 Data Portraits

Description

I extracted my sleep schedule from the Health app, and transform my length of sleep into circle size to see my sleeping habit over time.

Design Process

I started by making a nested loop by day and week, and spit the data in to lines, the way a normal calander is made.

Reflections

I think the hardest part of this project I struggled with was to get the informations in the array as text input. It took me a while to finally realize I should add "" and push a new text everytime.
